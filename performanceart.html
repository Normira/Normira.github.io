PERFORMANCE ART

As an interdisciplinary artist, I love the genre of performance art because of the freedom in regards to artistic tools. The most important thing is to have a concept, a starting point, a message you would like to express, or just an idea. And then you can do that in any way you want. Usually my performances are somehow always connected to music, singing and music improvisation. And I have the tendency to create some sort of plot or a narrative. And with my Bachelor's thesis being about the contemporary interactive stage performances, I couldn't help but create one of my own!

ROOTS - LIFE AS A BINGO GAME

Interactive Performance with music improvisation
SOFIA FUSION ART FEST 2016


CONCEPT: NoomRa
PARTICIPANTS: NoomRa, Zhulieta Ivanova, Konstantin Kuchev and the Audience
MUSIC: Konstantin Kuchev and NoomRa 



Life is like a game of Bingo - except you don't get to choose your number. 

Sometimes things happen and we think it's a coincidence, but it's not. It can't be. Because we are all interconnected: our lives, the planet, the universe. It is all one.
I believe in synchronicity. Do you?
