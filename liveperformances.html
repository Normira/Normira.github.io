UNDER THE SUNSET - QUARANTINE LIVE 

Lisbon, Portugal, 2020

I was invited to take part in the festivities for April 25th, organized by COLECTIVO A POSTOS (a Portuguese NGO). They decided to celebrate Portugal's national 'festa' with month-long online (due to Covid-19) programme. 
Big thanks to Sebastian Kleff for VJ-ing and being behind the camera!

*April 25th, known as Freedom Day, is a national holiday in Portugal. It celebrates the 1974 Revolution that ended the 50-year long dictatorship and started a democracy.

JAMTRONIC PROJECT LIVE 

Together with Marta Olaso  (a sound engineer, musician and DJ) from Spain, I had the pleasure to present our experimental project in Sofia, Bulgaria.
In 2015 Marta and me found each other, with the help of Lili Koss. We discovered that we enjoy improvising together - she would create experimental sound and I would sing along. It was pure magic! 
We performed together twice: at the event In the North Pole there are no angles (На Северния полюс няма ъгли) and at Skylab Mini Art Fest.

JAMMIN'

Improvising freely with strangers, who become friends. Because MUSIC UNITES!

Sofia, Bulgaria, 2016

Jamming has been a very important part of my life. I'm not a cover-performer, but an improviser. And the magic of creating music on the spur of the moment together with other musicians is something sacred for me and an incomparable learning experience. 

Different stages or places. Different musicians, which often I've never even met before. Different countries. Different instruments or tech gadgets...
Freely singing and merging with the sound - it makes me feel ALIVE and so complete!
The magic of being in tune with the hearts of the musicians around you and with your own heart and spirit!
I love that and I want to share it with you!
