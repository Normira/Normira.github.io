My digital collages are based on deconstructing and reassembling a photo, in order to form a new structure. It's a way of digital upcycling, which creates a new scope of existence of the original photo-predecessor. 
 All collages are made with photos, taken and edited by me, except for FLOWER POWER (photo of me, taken by Sebastian Kleff) and SUN, SAND, SEA (which includes a photo of me, taken by Denitsa Ganeva).
